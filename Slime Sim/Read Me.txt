Controls:

Left click / right click to attract / repel.

Shift + left/right to place / delete.

Scroll wheel to change mouse size (this effects the strength of the repeling / attraction and also lets you delete more or less slime.)

Scroll wheel + 1 to alter how strong the steering strength of the slime are.

Scroll wheel + 2 to alter how strong the random walking of the slime is.

To run: Double click SlimeSim.exe (if it doesn't work you need java 8.)
